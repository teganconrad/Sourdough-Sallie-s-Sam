Plain Sourdough Bread

This is the recipe you will use for all recipes!

Ingredients

  • 510g Bread Flour
  • 390g Water (Lukewarm)
  • 100g Sourdough Starter (Active)
  • 10g Salt

Make sure your sourdough starter is active and bubbly before using it in the recipe.

Instructions

This is all to be done by hand with either a wooden spoon or spatula.

  1. Combine your sourdough starter with luke warm water and stir until well combined.

    It is important that your starter is at it's peak before using it

  2. Mix the flour and salt in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Pour the water and starter mixture into the flour mixture and stir until a shaggy dough forms.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for one hour.

    Note: This is not for the dough to rise, but for the gluten to begin to develop.

  5. After the dough has rested, perform your first of fourstretch and folds.
    1. To perform a stretch and fold, grab one part of the dough, stretch it upwards and fold it back onto itself.
    2. Repeat this process for all four sides of the dough.
    3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Once all stretch and folds are complete, cover the dough and let it rise for 3 to 7 hours depending on your location and ambient temperature.

    Tip: If your house is cool, you can put it in the oven with the light on. Generally, if it is about 65 degrees it will take about 6 hours.

  7. Once the dough has about doubled you then begin shaping it.

    This will be similar to stretch and folds. This is also the part of where you can add in additional ingredients. You can refer to the Unique Bread section for add ins.

    You can also watch how to here:

    1. Lightly flour the top of the dough and flip it onto a clean surface.
    2. Gently stretch the dough into a rectangle.
    3. Fold the top edge down towards the center and seal it.
    4. Fold the side edges toward the center and seal it.
    5. Fold the bottom edge up towards the center and seal it.
    6. Take each side and fold them towards the center.
    7. Grab the arms and work them towards the center.
